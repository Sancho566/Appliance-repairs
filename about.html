About ApplianceRepair

Our Story

Founded in 2008, ApplianceRepair began as a small family-owned business with a single van and a commitment to excellence. What started as a local service in New York City has grown into a nationally recognized repair company serving both residential and commercial clients across 15 states.

Our founder, John Smith, a certified master technician with over 25 years of experience, established the company on three core principles: technical expertise, transparent pricing, and unparalleled customer service. Today, we maintain those values while embracing the latest technologies in appliance repair.
